"Hello! We're honored to assist you in understanding your health better. Our goal is to provide compassionate and reliable information every step of the way."

DxNow is a website that helps people check for leukemia early. You upload a picture of your blood cells, and DxNow uses computers to guess if there's a chance you have leukemia , what stage it might be, and what tests might help you figure it out for sure.

To use DxNow, you'll need a picture of your blood cells from a lab. Then, go to the DxNow website and upload the picture in the marked area. The website will then try to predict if leukemia is present, and what steps to take next. Keep in mind that DxNow is not intended to serve as a final diagnosis but will give you the idea if you need to consult a doctor for a proper diagnosis.

The techstack used is Java Spring for backend along with MySQL server to store User data like there credentials and the images provided by them.
